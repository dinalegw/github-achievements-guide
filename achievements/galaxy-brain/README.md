# Galaxy Brain

## Overview

Galaxy Brain celebrates high-impact contributions that improve project quality, workflow, or understanding. It rewards thoughtful, strategic changes.

## Why it matters

This badge shows you can think critically and deliver smart improvements. It highlights contributions that are not just functional, but also elegant and efficient.

## Requirements

* Propose a creative improvement or quality enhancement.
* Add documentation, testing, automation, or process improvements.
* Explain why your approach improves the project.

## Step-by-step

1. Review the repository and identify friction points.
2. Propose a solution that makes the project easier to use or maintain.
3. Open a PR with tests, docs, or a workflow update.
4. Document why the change matters in your PR.
5. Gather feedback and refine the implementation.

## Example

* Add a reproducible test for a common bug.
* Create a GitHub Action to automate releases or linting.
* Refactor a complex component into smaller, better-documented pieces.

## Success checklist

* Your change improves maintainability or automation.
* The PR includes clear context and justification.
* You used evidence or examples to explain the benefit.
* The update is consistent with the project's style and values.

## Tips

* Focus on clarity, not complexity.
* Use tests and documentation to support your change.
* Share your reasoning so maintainers understand the value.
