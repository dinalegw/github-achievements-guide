# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in this project safe for everyone.

## Our Standards

* Be respectful and considerate.
* Use welcoming language.
* Be collaborative and patient.
* Avoid harassment or exclusion.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue and tagging the maintainers.

## Enforcement

We reserve the right to remove contributions or block users who violate these standards.
