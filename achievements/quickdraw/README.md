# Quickdraw

## Overview

Quickdraw celebrates timely contributions that help a repository move forward quickly. It is about responsiveness, helpful feedback, and fast follow-through.

## Why it matters

Being responsive makes you a stronger collaborator. This achievement shows project maintainers you can act quickly and add momentum to open issues and reviews.

## Requirements

* Respond rapidly to an issue or pull request.
* Offer a useful fix, review, or contribution with minimal delay.
* Keep your response concise and action-oriented.

## Step-by-step

1. Follow active repositories and monitor new issues or PRs.
2. Identify a contribution you can make quickly.
3. Comment with a clear solution or open a small PR.
4. Update the contribution quickly when maintainers respond.
5. Close the loop by confirming the work with the project team.

## Example

* Spot a missing label or broken example in a PR and submit a small correction within hours.
* Provide a focused review comment with next steps and a suggested patch.

## Success checklist

* Your response is fast and relevant.
* You added value with a practical suggestion or fix.
* The issue or PR remained clear after your comment.
* You followed up when asked for clarification.

## Tips

* Use notifications and watch lists to stay informed.
* Keep your tone positive and supportive.
* When possible, include a small code or documentation change rather than only commentary.
